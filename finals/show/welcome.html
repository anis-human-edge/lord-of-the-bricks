Quick round of names — first name, what we did on this project. Two seconds each. Smiles, eye contact, no scripts.

Speaker note: Order matters. Start with whoever's calmest. End with whoever has the strongest line. Practice this until it feels natural — it's the first observable Core Values moment of the day.
04Icebreaker
For the judges

One thing we want you to ask us.

A single, generous opening — invite the judges to ask the question we most want to answer. It signals confidence and warmth, and it costs nothing.

Draft line: "We brought one thing each that we'd love to be asked about. So if anything we say sparks a question — please interrupt us. We'd much rather have a conversation than a monologue."
